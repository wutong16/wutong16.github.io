Biography

Tong Wu is a final-year Ph.D. student in Multi-Media Lab(MMLab) at CUHK, supervised by Professor Dahua Lin. She also works closely with Prof. Ziwei Liu at NTU. She is currently a visiting student researcher in Stanford University, working with Gordon Wetzstein. She got her bachelor’s degree in the EE Department at Tsinghua University in 2020, working with Prof. Yu Wang and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. Her research interests include but not limited to 3D Generation and Reconstruction.
