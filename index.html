Tong WU 吴桐

Ph.D. Student

Biography

Tong Wu is a Ph.D. student in Multi-Media Lab(MMLab) at CUHK, supervised by Professor Dahua Lin. She also works closely with Prof. Ziwei Liu at NTU. She got her bachelor’s degree in the EE Department at Tsinghua University in 2020, working with Prof. Yu Wang and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. Her research interests include but not limited to 3D Vision, Long-tailed Recognition, and Robustness.
